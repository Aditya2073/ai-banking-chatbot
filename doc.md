**1. INTRODUCTION**
In today’s digital era, banks are moving towards automation to improve
customer service and reduce manual workload.
AnAI Based Banking Chatbot System provides instant responses to customer queries
such as balance enquiry, transaction details, and general banking services.
This project aims to design and develop a smart banking chatbot that can assist
customers as well as bank staff efficiently.
**2. PROBLEM STATEMENT**
Traditional banking systems require customers to visit branches or wait
on customer care calls, which is time-consuming and inefficient.
There is a need for an automated system that can:
Provide 24×7 banking assistance
Reduce staff workload
Improve customer satisfaction
**3. OBJECTIVES OF THE PROJECT,**
To develop an AI-based chatbot for banking services
To provide secure login for customers and bank staff
To allow balance checking and transaction viewing
To simulate real banking operations digitally
To create a user-friendly web and mobile-friendly interface
**4. SCOPE OF THE PROJECT**
